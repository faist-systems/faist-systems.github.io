ABOUT FAIST

Why FAIST exists

FAIST was created from the need to understand technology as a whole, not as a collection of isolated tools. We focus not only on what technology can do, but primarily on how it fits into the system in which we live.

System thinking

Artificial intelligence, energy, security, and education are not separate worlds. FAIST connects them into a single framework, where each part makes sense only in relation to the others.

From experiment to practice

Research without application is blind. Implementation without understanding is dangerous. FAIST stands between these two poles and translates complex technologies into practical reality.

An open process

FAIST is not a finished product. It is a living system that evolves alongside technologies, people, and the questions we ask. This website is its map.
